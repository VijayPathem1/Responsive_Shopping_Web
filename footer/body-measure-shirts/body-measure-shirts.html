Neck Around

With the help of a cloth measuring tape, measure around the base of your neck, where shirt collar would normally sit, below your Adams Apple. Allow enough room for one finger between the measuring tape and your neck while taking measurement

Shoulder Width

Stand up straight in a relaxed posture With the help of a cloth measuring tape, measure the shoulder from left/right edge, where the yoke of the shirt starts / tips of shoulders. Start the end of the measuring tape on the outside edge of the shoulder, where you want the yoke of the shirt to start. This is where the horizontal part of the shoulder meets the vertical part of the arm. Run the measuring tape over the curve of the shoulders to the outside edge of the other shoulder

Chest Around

Stand up in your normal posture. Measure around the fullest part of your chest. Position the cloth measuring tape high up under the arms and keep it horizontal. Do not puff out your chest or arch your back in any unnatural pose

Waist Around

Stand up in your natural relaxed pose. Around 1‚Äù above the navel, measure around the fullest part of your waist. Make sure the measuring tape is snug on the skin but not squeezing in.

Biceps Around

With the help of a cloth measuring tape, measure around your biceps at its widest point. Make sure the tape measure is not too tight and lies close to the arm pit.

Wrist Around

Measure around the wrist bone. Hold the tape close to your skin

Sleeve Length

Measure your arm sideways by placing the cloth measuring tape from the tip of the shoulder. Hang the tape down to your hand. Measure till the point at wrist till the first metacarpal. Make sure measurements are taken without bending of the arm. As per your desired length for a short sleeve shirt, measure to a point on the external side of the arm.

Shirt Length

Measure the shirt length by placing the cloth measuring tape at the base of your neck and down to your back where you would determine the shirt length to be. In case of tucking the shirt, go marginally longer or shorter if you plan to wear the shirt untucked.

Body Images

Also send us your body images from front, back and both sides. The camera should be at shoulder height. Ideally, wear something that fits close to your body so that we can understand the body structure better
