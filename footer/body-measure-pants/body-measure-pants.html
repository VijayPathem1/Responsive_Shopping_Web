On the basis of your body measurements, we will make pants to achieve the desired fit Along with these measurements, kindly share images of front, back and side while wearing ant tight fit cloth to understand the shape of the body. Do not crop the images

Body Waist

Hold the cloth measurement tape around your waist. Keep the tape very close
to your skin. Note down the measurement.

Body Hip

Hold the cloth measurement tape around the fullest part of your hip/seat area.
Keep the measuring tape very near to your skin. Do not squeeze too much.
Note down the measurement.

Pant Length

Stand straight on the ground. Using a cloth measuring tape, measure the
length from your waist till the floor. Do not bend. This measurement gives you
the pant length, also called the outseam.

Body Inseam

Standing straight on the ground, using a cloth measuring tape, measure the
length from the crotch till the floor. Do not bend

Body Rise

Place the cloth measuring tape around the rise from the top of the desired
front waist line to the back of the waist line. The tape held should not be too
tight or too loose

Body Thigh

Measure your thigh, holding the cloth measuring tape 2‚Äù below the crotch.
Hold the tape around your thigh, keeping it close to your skin

Body Knee

Hold the cloth measuring tape around your knee, keeping it at the middle of
your knee. Keep the tape very close to your skin. Do not squeeze too much.

Body Ankle

Hold the cloth measuring tape around your ankle, keeping it at the middle of
your ankle. Keep the tape very close to your skin. Do not squeeze too much.

Body Images

Also send us your body images from front, back and both sides. The camera
should be at shoulder height.
Ideally , wear something that fits close to your body so that we can understand
the body structure better.
